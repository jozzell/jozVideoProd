Spinney Hill, African American History of Manhasset and Great Neck
The African American History of Manhasset and Great Neck is a vast and dynamic history. Here put together in over twenty interviews is the story of one of Long Island's best kept secrets; The Spinney Hill Story. Discover a history told by those who have witnessed and influenced some remarkable milestones and challenging losses. Produce by Dedrick Johnson and Lloyd Means (LMeans@Optonline.com)
